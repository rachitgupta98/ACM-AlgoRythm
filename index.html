About The Event


HACKATHON

Madhav Institute of Technology and Science (MITS) being one of the most prestigious engineering colleges in the heartland state Madhya Pradesh requires no introduction. Our aim at MITS, is to innovate technology for society and in order to do so, a Hackathon is always a wonderful idea.
A hackathon is an event that gathers programmers with different skill sets to “hack”, or work collaboratively, on a software project.

We believe that students should be aware of the current scenarios around the globe and therefore should be solving problems that deal with the current areas of problems that the companies are tackling throughout the world.
A problem set will be provided by the company to be solved at the Hackathon.
